Welcome to the MasterMind

Rules

The rules are simple, you have to guess the sequence of 5 numbers generated by the computer. After giving your input, the program will let you know which numbers are correct by showing a sequence. If you guessed a number correctly, it will show you a white circle. If you guessed a number that appears in the sequence but at the wrong location, a black circle will be displayed. Lastly, you will not get any "feedback circle" if the number is wrong.

You will have the option between a sequence of number or a sequence of colours. For the number option, use the "Enter" key to submit your guess. Furthermore, you can choose between 6 levels:

1 - No time limit, no duplicate in the numbers

2 - Time limit, no duplicate in the numbers

3 - No time limit, duplicate in the numbers

4 - Time limit, duplicate in the numbers

5 - With a maximum number of steps, no duplicate in the numbers

6- With a maximum number of steps, duplicate in the numbers


As seen in the photos, you will enter your guesses at the bottom of the page.


Good Luck!
